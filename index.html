System

The sealed chamber

Circulation fans and overhead mist keep the grow space uniform—without opening the crop to shared greenhouse air.

Breathing containers

Nested dual-cylinders rotate to align aeration slots. Mesh bottoms drain. Roots get exactly the airflow they need.

Direct-to-root cooling

Evaporator coils wrap each sleeve. An inverter compressor modulates the cycle—cooling the biological epicenter, not empty rooms.

Closed-loop water

Mist falls, returns to the reservoir, filters, and recirculates—thermally conditioned to avoid root shock.

Biosecurity array

UV and spectrum sanitizers keep air, water, and surfaces clean—chemical-free pathogen defense built into the cabinet.
